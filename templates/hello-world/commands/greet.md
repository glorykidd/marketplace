# Greet

Say hello to the provided name.

## INPUTS

- Name argument — who or what to greet (default: `World` if not provided)

## INSTRUCTIONS

1. Read the argument. If none was provided, use `World`.
2. Respond with a friendly greeting addressed to that name.
3. Include one sentence about why it's great to meet them.

## OUTPUT FORMAT

A single short greeting, no headers or lists needed.

Example:
> Hello, World! It's wonderful to meet you.
